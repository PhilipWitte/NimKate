Installation
---
- **Syntax**: Copy `nimrod.xml` to `~/.kde4/share/apps/katepart/syntax/`
- **Indent**: Copy `nimrod.js` to `~/.kde4/share/apps/katepart/script/indentation/`
- **Colors**: Kate > Settings > Configure Kate > Fonts & Colors > (Schema) Import...
